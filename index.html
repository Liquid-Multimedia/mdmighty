About The Duo

Hailing from 'The Northern Rivers' NSW, Mark David & The Mighty Quinn are accomplished musicians in their own right, teaming up on Acoustic Guitar, Drums & Vocals.

They create a fun & dynamic duo covering music from the Delta Blues to Dylan, Neil Young, Hendrix, Oz Rock & current classics guaranteeing a fun evening of live music.

The Duo is versatile at being able to play the appropriate music for all settings. Their wealth of experience shows in their performances & their ability to connect to an audience.
